French Fries

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2-3 servings
Ingredients Quantity
potato, cut into evenly sized strips 1 large russet
vegetable oil for frying 2 cups
salt as needed

Step 1: Soak potato strips in a large bowl of water for about 30 minutes.
Step 2:Pat with paper towels until thoroughly dry.
Step 3: Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Gently add potatoes to the hot oil and fry for about 5 minutes, stirring and flipping the potatoes occasionally.
Step 4: Use a slotted spoon to transfer potatoes to a paper towel-lined plate. Let cool completely.
Step 5:Heat oil again, but this time to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Add potatoes and fry a second time until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes, take out and enjoy!

Potato Chips

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2-3 servings
Ingredients Quantity
potato, thinly sliced 1 large russet
vegetable oil for frying 2 cups
salt as needed

Step 1: Thinly slice the potatoes using a mandoline or a very sharp knife, cutting them to a thickness between 1/8-inch and 1/16-inch.
Step 2:Transfer the sliced potatoes to an ice bath (prepared by simply filling a large bowl with ice and water). Soak in the cold water for 10 minutes.
Step 3: Drain the potato slices and arrange them on a paper towel-lined baking sheet in a single layer. Lay more paper towels on top and pat the potatoes well to dry them off.
Step 4: Fill a large Dutch oven with 2 inches of vegetable oil and set over medium-high heat. Add a deep-fry thermometer and wait until the oil reaches 325Â°F. Fry the sliced potatoes in batches. Stir occasionally until the slices are light brown, which takes about 3 minutes.
Step 5:Place a wire rack inside a baking sheet and line it with paper towels. Remove the chips from the oil with a slotted spoon and place them on the paper towels and enjoy!
